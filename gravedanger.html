Grave Danger

In 2016 I produced Grave Danger through JB Gaming Inc with a small team. The game is inspired by SNES classics such as The Lost Vikings, Mega Man X, and Castlevania. It is now available on Steam and will be available on Xbox One by Summer 2017.




My role as producer and lead developer consisted of scheduling and coordinating artists, programming all features, building the entire game in Unity3D, and launching on Steam.
